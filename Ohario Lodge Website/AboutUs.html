Keith and Janet, are the owners and hosts of the Ohario Lodge. They offer a wealth of experience in hospitality to ensure an unforgettable guest experience.

With a deep appreciation for the region's natural beauty and a passion for sharing it with others, Keith and Janet have created an oasis of comfort and luxury in the heart of New Zealand's South Island wilderness.

Keith's expertise in outdoor adventures, combined with Janet's culinary finesse, make for a perfect team to provide an enriching and personalized stay for all who visit their idyllic retreat.
