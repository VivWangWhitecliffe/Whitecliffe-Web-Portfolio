FAQs

SHIPMENT: How long will it take for my order to arrive?

Typically the order is packed and sent away from Auckland the next day (of purchase). If you are within NZ, you should receive it within 5 - 10 business days.
You will receive an NZ Post tracking number if you chose priority shipping, in which case you will be able to see updates on the NZ Post website/ app.

PAYMENT: How to pay for my order?

As soon as you enter all your details and submit it, we will send you an email with an option of paying via Paypal or direct debit. We will include our bank details in the email as well. Once we get a confirmation of payment, your order will be on it's way!

CUSTOM DESIGN: How do I request a custom design?

Pop in to out 'CONTACT' section and send us a message with a descriptive message, you can include a picture as well. We will be in touch to create your perfect piece of jewellery/ item!

DAMAGES & EXCHANGES: How do I cancel, exchange or return my order?

Pop in to out 'CONTACT' section and send us a message with a descriptive message of what happened to the item, preferrably with a picture. We will be in touch to sort your order out ASAP.

WRONG ADDRESS: I typed in the wrong address, how do I change it?

If it is the same day as you placed the order, we might be able to redirect your order. However, if it has been over 24 hours after placing the order, you will need to contact NZ Post.
Alternatively, you could get in touch with us for a separate order and we will see how we can best help you!

JEWELLERY CARE: How to care for my jewellery?

1. Avoid contact with perfumes and body lotions as it may dull your jewellery.
2. Avoid wearing your jewellery while swimming, exercising and showering etc.
3. Give it a gentle wipe with a damp cloth every now and again.
Help your jewellery live their best life!
